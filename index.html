Our Story

Three decades of educational excellence, countless success stories, and an unwavering commitment to nurturing future leaders.

A Legacy of Excellence

Founded in 1992, Riara School began as a vision to create an educational institution that would not only impart knowledge but shape character, inspire creativity, and foster a lifelong love for learning. What started as a small community school has grown into one of Kenya's most respected educational institutions.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to excellence, and a deep understanding that every child is unique. We believe that education is not just about academic achievement, but about developing well-rounded individuals who will contribute meaningfully to society.

Today, we stand proud as a beacon of educational excellence, having graduated thousands of students who have gone on to become leaders, innovators, and changemakers in their respective fields. Our legacy is built on the foundation of our core values and the dedication of our exceptional faculty and staff.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that drive everything we do at Riara School

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops critical thinking, creativity, and character in every student. We are committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals who are academically prepared, morally grounded, and globally minded, ready to make meaningful contributions to society.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in East Africa, recognized for excellence in holistic education, innovation in teaching methodologies, and developing future leaders who will positively impact their communities and the world at large.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how we protect and handle your personal information.

Last Updated: December 2024

At Riara School, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information about students, parents, staff, and visitors when you interact with our school, use our website, or engage with our services.

Important Notice

This policy applies to all personal data processed by Riara School, including data collected through our website, mobile applications, and during your interactions with our school community.

What Information We Collect

Student Information

  • Personal details (name, date of birth, address, contact information)
  • Academic records and performance data
  • Attendance records and behavioral information
  • Medical information and emergency contacts
  • Photos and videos for educational and promotional purposes
  • Financial information related to school fees and payments

Parent/Guardian Information

  • Contact details and identification information
  • Communication preferences and records
  • Financial information for fee payment purposes
  • Emergency contact information

Website and Digital Information

  • IP address and browser information
  • Cookies and usage data
  • Form submissions and inquiries
  • Login credentials for online portals

Sensitive Information

We may collect sensitive information such as medical records, learning difficulties, or special needs information solely for the purpose of providing appropriate care and educational support.

How We Use Your Information

Educational Purposes

  • Providing educational services and curriculum delivery
  • Monitoring academic progress and performance
  • Arranging educational trips and extracurricular activities
  • Providing pastoral care and support services
  • Managing special educational needs and disabilities

Administrative Purposes

  • Student enrollment and registration processes
  • Fee collection and financial management
  • Communication with parents and guardians
  • Attendance tracking and reporting
  • Maintaining school records and databases

Safety and Security

  • Ensuring student safety and wellbeing
  • Emergency contact and medical care
  • Safeguarding and child protection
  • Security monitoring and access control

Legal and Regulatory Compliance

  • Meeting statutory reporting requirements
  • Compliance with education regulations
  • Supporting legal proceedings if necessary
  • Responding to lawful requests from authorities

Data Sharing & Third Parties

When We Share Information

We may share your personal information with third parties only in the following circumstances:

Educational Partners

  • Examination boards and educational authorities
  • Other schools during student transfers
  • Educational service providers and consultants
  • Library and learning resource providers

Service Providers

  • IT service providers and cloud storage companies
  • Payment processing companies
  • Transportation and catering services
  • Marketing and communication platforms

Legal Requirements

  • Government agencies and regulatory bodies
  • Law enforcement agencies when required by law
  • Child protection services
  • Court orders and legal proceedings

Data Protection Measures

All third parties we work with are required to implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data and use it only for the specified purposes.

Your Rights

Under data protection law, you have several rights regarding your personal information:

Access Rights

  • Right to be informed: You have the right to know how your data is being used
  • Right of access: You can request copies of your personal data
  • Right to rectification: You can ask us to correct inaccurate information

Control Rights

  • Right to erasure: You can request deletion of your data in certain circumstances
  • Right to restrict processing: You can limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: You can request your data in a portable format
  • Right to object: You can object to certain types of processing

How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@riaraschool.ac.ke
  • Phone: +254 123 456 789
  • Post: Data Protection Officer, Riara School, P.O. Box 1234-00100, Nairobi

Response Timeline

We will respond to your request within 30 days. If we need more time, we'll let you know and explain why.

Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better browsing experience and allow certain features to work properly.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

Cookie Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can change your cookie preferences at any time through your browser settings.
